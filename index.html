About Me

I was born way back in 1992. This year was particularly important to Kenyan politics as it's the year when the first multi-party elections were held; since our Independence in 1963.

I was born in Nairobi, the first-born in my family.

We moved around a few times in my infancy and early toddler ages but finally settled in Nairobi's outskirts: a place known as Thindigua, near Kiambu Town.

My parents really don't like that name "Thindigua". The area is alternatively known as Windsor Grove.


One of my earliest interests was music. I love music. It's what I do effortlessly. Trust me on this.

I've also always been fascinated with Computer/Video Games. This fascination almost reaches levels of addiction.
